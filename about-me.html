Mr. Scales C. Fishman

Portfolio

  • Two Gills Magazine: War Photographer January 2000-Present

    • As the Great Tetrapod Wars broke out across the freshwater deltas in 2001, Mr. Fishman captured the now-famous portrait of a young trout, fatally-injured by rebels while escaping from his reef. These harrowing images brought the marine-world's attention to the crisis in Hood River. He documented the trout's last minutes.

  • Guppy Fish Care: Web Developer September 1998-January 2000

    • Developed a multi-page news site for guppyfishcare.com, the world's #1 source for, well, taking care of guppy's.

  • The New York Times: Cartoonist August 1994-September 1998

    • Nominated for the Pulitzer Prize for thought-provoking weeklys on life as a marine-dwelling creature.

  • Wegman's Grocery: Fish Model December 1980

    • Was on display in the fresh fish department, until a mad scientist brought me back to life by attaching my head to a human body.
