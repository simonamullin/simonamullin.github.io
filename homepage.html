Mr. Scales C. Fishman

Half-Man, Half-Fish - Full Heart

Since crawling out from the neon waters of the raging Willamette River in 1989, Mr. Fishman has become a household name in cross-specie awareness, fish-based rights initiatives, and your child's waking nightmares. Mr. Fishman is currently an underwater war photographer for Fin-lands famous "Two Gills" magazine, and is most recognizable for his work covering the Tetropod Wars in the early 2000's.
